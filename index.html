Howard Zhang

I am a 3rd year PhD student at UCLA where I am advised by Prof. Achuta Kadambi. My research interests include computer vision and computational imaging, particularly in the blending of physics and AI.

I attended UC Berkeley for my undergrad where I was fortunate to work with Professor Kristofer Pister on robotics and AI.

Enhancing Diffusion Models with 3D Perspective Geometry Constraints
Rishi Upadhyay, Howard Zhang, Yunhao Ba, Ethan Yang, Blake Gella, Sicheng Jiang, Alex Wong, Achuta Kadambi
SIGGRAPH Asia, 2023
PDF

WeatherStream: Light Transport Automation of Single Image Deweathering
Howard Zhang, Yunhao Ba, Ethan Yang, Varan Mehra, Blake Gella, Akira Suzuki, Arnold Pfahnl, Chethan Chinder Chandrappa, Alex Wong, Achuta Kadambi
CVPR, 2023
PDF

We introduce a pipeline that uses the power of light-transport physics to collect a large dataset of paired clear and weathered frames to train a SOTA model on weather removal.

Paper
